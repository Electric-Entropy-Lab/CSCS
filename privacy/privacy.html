Privacy Policy

Cognitive State Control System (CSCS Lite)
Version: Lite / Research Prototype
Last updated: 2026-02-08

1. Overview

CSCS Lite is a browser extension designed as a research and self-observation tool. Its purpose is to reflect interaction patterns that may indicate cognitive load or loss of executive control during knowledge work.

CSCS Lite is not a productivity tool, medical application, diagnostic system, or behavioral optimization service.

2. Data Processing Principles

CSCS Lite operates under the following non-negotiable principles:

  • Local-only data processing
  • No external data transmission
  • No cloud storage
  • No analytics or tracking services
  • No advertising or profiling

3. Data Collected

The extension observes interaction signals generated during browser usage. These signals are used to compute local, time-based metrics.

The collected signals may include:

  • Keyboard interaction timing (e.g. key press intervals, key hold duration)
  • Use of correction keys (e.g. backspace, delete)
  • Mouse interaction dynamics (movement, clicks)
  • Focus and blur events
  • Scroll activity
  • Session timing and activity continuity

Important: CSCS Lite does not record, analyze, or interpret the semantic content of text, websites, messages, or conversations.

4. What Is Explicitly Not Collected

  • Text content or meaning
  • Passwords or form values
  • Website content or page data
  • Personal identifiers
  • Account information
  • Location data

5. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the user’s device. Depending on browser capabilities and user consent, data may be stored using:

  • IndexedDB
  • Local browser storage
  • User-selected local file system locations

CSCS Lite does not maintain any external servers and does not transmit data outside the user’s browser.

6. Data Export and Deletion

The user remains in full control of their data.

  • Data can be exported manually as structured files (e.g. JSON)
  • Local data can be deleted at any time by the user
  • No data persists outside the user’s environment

7. Permissions

CSCS Lite requires certain browser permissions in order to function correctly:

  • activeTab – to observe interaction signals in the active tab
  • storage – to store data locally
  • scripting – to inject the content script
  • contextMenus – to allow manual user tagging
  • notifications – to display optional local alerts

These permissions are used strictly for local operation and are not associated with any external services.

8. No Medical or Psychological Claims

CSCS Lite does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any medical or psychological condition. Any indicators presented by the system are probabilistic signals intended for self-reflection and research purposes only.

9. Third-Party Services

CSCS Lite does not use:

  • Third-party analytics
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking pixels
  • External APIs

10. Changes to This Policy

If the data processing model of CSCS Lite changes in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
